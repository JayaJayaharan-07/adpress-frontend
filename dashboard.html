Choose Ad Format

Understanding the difference helps you pick the best format for your budget and goal

Classified Ad

Text-only ads that appear in the classified section of the newspaper. Charged per word, line, or character. Appears alongside similar category ads. Cost-effective for high volumes.

Most Popular Budget Friendly

Display Ad

Visual ads with images, logos, custom typography, and creative designs. Can appear anywhere in the newspaper. Charged per column centimeter (cc) or per page fraction. High visibility.

High Impact Brand Building
